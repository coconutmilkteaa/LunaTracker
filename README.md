what it does

tracks your cycle, predicts the next one

log mood, energy, sleep, cramps, skin, flow, notes

moodboard that shifts with your phase

vision board you can fill with your own pics + gifs

charts to spot patterns over time

5 palettes, light/dark mode, vibe slider

privacy

everything lives in your browser's localStorage. no server, no analytics, no account needed. i can't see your data, no one can. 
flip side: clear your browser data and it's gone, so export every once in a while.
getting started
easiest way → just open the live site.
wanna keep it offline? download index.html, double-click. that's the whole install.

your data

DATA card at the bottom has export (.json or .csv) and import buttons. use them to back up or move stuff between devices/browsers.

tech

vanilla js + chart.js. one html file. no frameworks, no build step, no npm.
